Experience next generation banking with secure payments, instant transfers, smart analytics and AI powered financial tools — all from your mobile.

Our Key Features

Zero Balance Savings Account

Open your Stackly account instantly without visiting a branch. Complete video KYC in minutes and receive a virtual debit card immediately. No minimum balance and no paperwork required.

Users can manage their savings, view transactions, download statements and control cards directly from the mobile app anytime.

Open Account

UPI & Instant Payments

Send or receive money instantly using UPI ID, mobile number or QR code. Stackly supports 24/7 transfers to any bank in India with real-time notifications.

Smart fraud detection protects every transaction and prevents suspicious activities automatically.

Try UPI

Instant Personal Loans

Apply for personal loans directly inside the app. Our AI credit scoring approves eligible users within minutes and funds are transferred immediately.

Flexible repayment plans, low interest rates and no hidden charges.

Apply Loan

Smart Expense Analytics

Track where your money goes. Stackly automatically categorizes expenses like food, shopping, travel and bills.

Monthly spending reports help users improve savings habits and financial planning.

View Insights

Advanced Security Protection

We use bank-grade encryption, biometric login, OTP verification and AI fraud monitoring to secure your money.

You can instantly block or unblock your debit card from the app.
