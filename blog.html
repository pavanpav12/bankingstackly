About Stackly Bank

Stackly Digital Bank is a next generation fintech platform designed to provide paperless banking services. Customers can open accounts instantly, transfer funds using UPI, apply for loans, and monitor expenses using AI analytics. Our mission is to make banking faster, safer, and accessible to everyone without visiting branches. We use bank-level encryption and fraud detection systems to ensure user safety.
