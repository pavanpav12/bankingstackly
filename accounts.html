Account Overview

Stackly savings account provides UPI payments, debit card, mobile banking and instant alerts with advanced security.

Stackly Digital Savings Account is a fully online banking solution designed for convenience, security and flexibility. Customers can open an account in minutes using Aadhaar and PAN verification without visiting a branch. The account provides UPI payments, NEFT/IMPS transfers, debit card access, mobile banking and real-time transaction alerts. With the Stackly mobile banking platform, customers can check balances, download statements, block or unblock cards, set spending limits and track expenses instantly. The system uses AI-based monitoring to detect unusual transactions and prevent fraud. All transactions are protected with multi-factor authentication and encrypted security protocols. The savings account is suitable for students, salaried employees, professionals and small business owners who want fast and paperless banking with 24/7 availability.

Why Choose Stackly

Zero Balance

No minimum balance requirement.

Stackly provides a zero minimum balance savings account so customers do not need to maintain any compulsory monthly balance. This makes banking accessible to students and new users who want to start saving without financial pressure.

Instant Activation

Account opens within minutes.

The account is activated within minutes using secure digital KYC verification. After successful verification, customers immediately receive account number, IFSC code, UPI ID and virtual debit card to start transactions instantly.

Secure Banking

Advanced encryption protection.

Stackly uses advanced security systems including 256-bit encryption, OTP verification, biometric login and AI fraud detection. Every transaction is monitored in real time to ensure safe and protected digital banking.
